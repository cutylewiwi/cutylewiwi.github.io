About

I am a fourth-year Ph.D. student at Opera Group and Sysnet Group at UC San Diego, working with Prof. Yuanyuan Zhou. I received my bachelor degree in Peking University. (In Chinese: 北京大学)

My research interests lie in systems reliability, security and usability. I majorily focus on providing OS support for novel hardware devices like Processing-in-Memory and Persistent Memory, and making user applications easily use these devices.
